Funk and Schuster Fine Art Printing

The Funk and Schuster studio produces high quality lithographs using aluminum offset plates on a 40" SORS Heidelberg, and a 55" Dufa for specialty work and large prints. Working one on one with the printer, artists have access to a unique variety of choices in format, size, and materials to create stunning art.
